Recipe for Chocolate Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  6. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake springs back when gently touched.
  8. Allow to cool before frosting with your favorite chocolate frosting.
